It's not everyone who will understand your path, but that's okay, it's your path.

My passion for programming began during the Systems Analysis and Development technical course at ETEC – Centro Paula Souza, in 2008. Although I worked mainly with web development, my curiosity for physiology and biochemistry led me to study Physical Education – Bachelor's degree. After completing my degree, I embarked on a Master's degree in Human Movement Sciences at UENP. During this time, I discovered my calling as a data scientist, combining my programming skills with my interest in science and statistics. Currently (2023) I am studying my second degree in Data Science at FATEC - Ourinhos, in the interior of the state of SP.
